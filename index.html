Purpose of this study.

The purpose of this was to examine the bacterial species found in the belly buttons of various test subjects in order to determine which one would be the most suitable to use in a meat substitute product. The user on this site is able to select each test subject from the dropdown below (identied by their ID number) in order to look at the top 10 bacterial species, as well as how often each test subject washed their belly button.
